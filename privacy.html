Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Usage

Absolute Immersion is designed to be a private focus companion. We believe your focus and time are yours alone. This app does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.

All focus sessions, timers, and interaction data are processed entirely locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks into Absolute Immersion. No data is sent to external servers.

3. Apple's Opt-in Analytics

If you have explicitly opted-in to share crash logs and usage data with app developers via your Apple device settings, we may receive anonymous crash reports and broad, aggregate usage statistics directly from Apple. This data cannot be linked to you personally and is used solely to fix bugs and improve the app.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Since we collect no personal information, any changes will simply reflect updates to our local app functionality or compliance requirements. The updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting.
